Bio

In 2016, PrEyX teamed up with eHash and created OTAKUpassport. Their goal was to combine their convention, nightclub, and production experience to innovate a new experience for anime fans.

PrEyX

  • Twitch
  • Facebook
  • Instagram
  • Twitter
  • Soundcloud

Based out of Southern California, PrEyX has been a DJ in the anime convention scene since 2011. He started staffing Anime Expo (AX) in 2005 and became the manager of ticketing from 2007 to 2009. In 2010, PrEyX joined the AX Live Broadcast Team and was a predominant face as a host that year. He started performing for Crunchyroll Booth/Dance Party at AX in 2013. You can catch PrEyX streaming Final Fantasy XIV on Twitch.

eHash

  • Twitch
  • Facebook
  • Instagram
  • Twitter
  • Soundcloud

As a kid, eHash attended his first convention in 1981 San Diego Comic Con. He has staffed or been a part of operations for Los Angeles Sci Fi and Comic Convention (Ticket Cashier), Anime Los Angeles (Dance and Food Truck Area), TouhouCon (Operations), FanimeCon (Dance Dept Head 2008 to 2018), Crunchyroll Expo (Performer (2017 to present).

eHash has been a professional DJ since 1999. Besides Nightclubs and Festivals, he has done Trade Shows, Corporate Launch Events, Corporate Holiday Parties, and private events. Previous clients include: Sun Microsystems, Oracle, Apple, Sony, EA, Verizon, Bandai, Hyve, Google, Facebook, Twitch/TwitchCon.

eHash is currently the Technical Director for Pure Nightclub (www.purenightclub408.com) in Sunnyvale and YOLO Nightclub (www.yolonightclub.com) in San Francisco. He is also the resident VJ at Pure Nightclub and Resident DJ at both clubs, opening almost every weekend for the Top DJs and acts from around the world.

Novae/Tetsu

  • Twitch
  • Discord
  • Instagram
  • Twitter

Novae, also known on Twitch as NovaeStorm, has been staffing anime conventions/exhibits since 2009. In 2013, she was given the task of managing the dance opartions by eHash. Since then, her role in any music event has been to be the on-site coordinator. With OTAKUpassport, she also takes on the role of producing or acquiring many visuals used with the production. During the convention, she is usually seen interacting with attendees and managing OTAKUpassport's interactive elements, both virtual and in-person. She occasionally streams variety games on Twitch and is Tetsu's owner.

Tetsu is a Husky and Alaskan Malamute mix (also known as alusky) born in February 2021. He is the company's living mascot and made his first appearance in Virtual Crunchyroll Expo 2021

itsDatGai

  • Twitch
  • Twitter

itsDatGai is the back-up DJ performer for OTAKUpassport as of 2021. He is currently a resident DJ and manager for AniCloverClub. His style of music is to mix meme oriented, AniSong/Anicore songs.

PeterBot

  • Instagram

PeterBot, also known as NoParties4Peter on Twitch, is the operations assistant when certain events/performances require additional crowd control or logistical resources. He joined OTAKUpassport in 2021 for Virtual Crunchyroll Expo, where his nickname "PeterBot" was coined.
